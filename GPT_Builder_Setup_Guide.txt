# GPT Builder Setup Guide — GPTOPT Gaming Assistant

1. Paste `instructions.txt` into the GPT Builder Instructions section.
2. Add prompts from `prompt_examples.json` under Conversation Starters.
3. Upload useful helper files from this repo as knowledge or reference material.
4. Use `Scripts/Ultimate-Utility.ps1` as an optional operational helper script.
5. Keep the GPT focused on gaming optimization, diagnostics, rollback, and validation.

Suggested welcome message:
"Welcome to GPTOPT Gaming Assistant — focused on gaming performance, latency, diagnostics, and rollback-aware optimization."
